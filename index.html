SERVICES

Our Services at VITMOL includes

DRUG DEVELOPMENT, DESIGN AND DISCOVERY (4D™)

Target Discovery

We help you to identify and validate the most significant targets using expression data (public or user provided) and also predict the ‘best-in-class’ targets as curated from existing literatures

Application: Target based Drug discovery, polypharmacology

Structure Based drug discovery

Screen as much as possible putative ligands from our database of over 50,000 drug-like compounds.
Predict and describe the best drug binding site..
Predict receptor-ligand molecule interaction for drug design.

Application: Drug discovery and optimization, Drug repurposing

Ligand Based drug Discovery

Mathematically model ligand bioactivity and contributing factors using cheminformatics tools.
Detection and Mapping of the core electronics and physicochemical properties associated with the bioactivity of a group of related ligands.

Application: Lead optimization, scaffold identification and generation.

DATABASE MANAGEMENT (DATMAN™)

Phytochemical Database Generation

We develop user friendly databases for your plant selection. Request for our herbarium to see our plant collection. If your plant is not present, we can help you build your database upon request.

Target specific Inhibitors

Request for a collection of ligands in their 1,2&3-D configurations of special targets. (Special targets include: kinase, GPCR)

Drug Repurposing study

Do you have a lead concerning the repurposing of a particular group of molecules towards another target? We can help you to develop your database

Decoy Databases

We can help you create Decoy Databases. Decoys are best likened to negative controls in wet lab experiments. They are useful in the final validation of your molecular docking experiments.

Complete Pipeline Resource

Want a complete bioinformatics resource set for a particular disease? From gene sequences to protein and ligands, we can build it for you in the most easily accessible mode.
